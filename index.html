Sirene

A Python package that facilitates access to raw greenhouse gas (GHG) emission data from Brazil’s “Sistema de Registro Nacional de Emissões” (SIRENE) by the Ministry of Science, Technology, and Innovation. It enables users to retrieve disaggregated emission data and estimate emission coefficients, supporting environmental economic analyses. https://pypi.org/project/sirene/

BenToTru

A Python package designed to estimate GHG emissions based on energy consumption in Brazil, categorized by 51 and 68 distinct economic activities. Utilizing data from the National Energy Balance (BEN) and emission factors adapted to Brazilian conditions, it provides detailed emissions by economic sector and energy product. https://pypi.org/project/BenToTru/
